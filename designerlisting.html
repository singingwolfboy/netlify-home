Lead Designer

About the Position

The traditional approach to making websites is being disrupted by new technologies. Static site generators, build automation tools and git are leading the way.

Netlify has rolled all the services needed to take your code, build it and host it on our CDN into a single automated platform. Creating modern, high performance websites and applications is simpler, cheaper and more secure than ever.

Started just a year ago, we’re already serving hundreds of millions page-views per month. We have a long list of clients like Instrument, WeWork and Uber, and we’ve helped thousands of developers build web applications.

We’re looking for a full-time designer to make Netlify better for developers, designers and anyone who creates for the web. You will be responsible for driving the product design strategy, branding and user experience. We’re still a small team, so being comfortable writing code for front-end applications is a huge plus.

We’re based in San Francisco, and we are looking for someone local, but we’ll consider remote employment for the right candidate.

Our Ideal Candidate

  • Wants to design for developers, and understands how to work with and build for them
  • Is an expert in HTML/CSS
  • Has experience with front-end development
  • Is familiar with the git-centric workflow
  • Can quickly iterate designs and ideas when needed
  • Has excellent written and oral communications with strong presentation skills
  • Is up to date regarding the latest web design and technology trends
  • Thills in an environment where minimal guidance, experimentation and failure are celebrated
  • Gives and receives feedback with regard for both emotional and technical concerns.

We Offer

  • Equity
  • Competitive salary
  • Benefits
  • Lunch, snacks and drinks
  • Brilliant co-workers with backgrounds at GitHub, Docker, Yelp, and more
  • A great office space in San Francisco’s Dogpatch
  • The opportunity to be part of a young team where you can leave your mark and help us change the fundamental way sites have been made for the last 15 years

Please read the qualifications for this position carefully. The successful applicant will have to get up to speed quickly and therefore, we will only consider those who meet all the criteria listed above.
